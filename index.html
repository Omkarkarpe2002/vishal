             We stayed here two nights as a party of four. We had booked the shanti hostel 2 but the management had lost our booking and were fully booked, so we got put into the overflow room in shanti hostel 1 with very bad and uncomfortable beds. One bed kept breaking and the bunks had a lean and dip to the right. My sheet didn't feel clean and didn't fit the bed, kept coming off! The staff did accommodate their mistake well by giving us 50% off and a bottle of wine. They were very apologetic and felt very bad for loosing our booking. There was only two bathrooms for appox 20 guests and a line up for them. Bathroom floors get very wet and sink drips onto floor splashing feet all the time. Great outdoor area. Very friendly and helpful staff. Perhaps we would stay here again...

         We arrived at the hostel in the afternoon and could easily book a room. Bunkbeds have good a mattresses, linen are clean and no bedbugs, however also no air conditioning. Lockers are not too large and fit only thin locks. Living room has a very nice couch, a tv and a playstation. Outside is a beautiful garden and swimming pool to relax. It is quite far from the city center (30 minute walk) but there are some beautiful vineyards close by that you can visit. The city itself is not super special, but there are some very nice bars, the people are very friendly and robbery is something unheard of. This place is great to relax and check out the surroundings. The local tourist information can give you a lot to do.
